Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

At ServeSYNC, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and Google account ID upon sign-in.
  • Service Data: Details of your submitted or tracked service hours.
  • Usage Data: Activity logs such as login timestamps and submission history.

How We Use Your Information

  • Manage and process service hour submissions and approvals.
  • Generate records for service recognition and award purposes.
  • Monitor system usage to improve functionality and support.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Only authorized Burnside High School staff may access your information to process and review service submissions.

Security

ServeSYNC uses industry-standard practices to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data. Please contact your school administrator to make such requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated via the ServeSYNC platform.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact the ServeSYNC administrator.
